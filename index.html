Who is Piper?

Hi, I'm Piper! I'm a 34 year old non binary person who has been dabbling in code for years, without ever realising I could turn my weird hobby into a career.

Before School of Code, I was working as a freelance illustrator, and have illustrated children's books, published comic strips, and worked for a bunch of companies designing brochuers. I love to draw and create, but the isolation of working for myself has never been ideal, and I would much rather work on my own personal projects rather than illustration work for other people.

I love working in teams with other creative people, collaborating on projects and coming up with new ideas, so when I saw School of Code was open for applicants, I knew this was the path for me!

More information about Piper

Outside of coding and drawing, I'm pretty much constantly getting myself into new things. I'm passionate about so many things. I'm very interested in social politics, especially LGBTQIA+ rights, gender equality, feminism, anti-racism and all that Social Justice Warrior stuff (although do consider myself more of a social justice bard).

In my free time, I'm likely to be found playing videogames or playing my ukulele and pretending I can sing. I live in Warwick with my partner, our housemate, and 6 pet rats!
